The Self Portrait

Watercolour

I adore portraiture as a form of self-expression, particularly when capturing the angst and frustration we all feel in our developmental years.

I created this piece to externalize how I felt after hurting someone I sincerely cared about. The pose represents utter embarrassment and regret but the bright colours show hope to change and the vibrancy of being true to yourself again.
