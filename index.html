A little about me...

In comp eng I'm passionate about problem-solving and creative design, which I apply in cryptography and architecture design in my internship at Marvell.

I love exploring the field of Aerospace as a Kit Rocket and Tech Dev member of the rocketry engineering team at Queen's as well as coordinating outreach to foster interest in the fields of Space and Engineering with the NASA's Human Exploration Rover Competition, happening this year in Alabama!

Outside of all the nerd stuff, my interests include painting when I can, seeing the local punk and drag scenes wherever I am, and playing club hockey and rugby.
