A little about me...

In comp eng I'm passionate about problem-solving and creative design, which I apply in cryptography and architecture in my internship at Marvell.

Outside of all the nerd stuff, my interests include painting when I can, seeing the local punk and drag scenes wherever I am, and playing club hockey and rugby.
